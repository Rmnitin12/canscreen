The science

Why one-size sunscreen
doesn't work

01

Fitzpatrick scale

Skin tone determines baseline UV sensitivity. Our AI classifies your skin from I–VI to calibrate SPF strength and mineral vs. chemical filter ratios.

02

UV index variance

UV changes with altitude, latitude, and ozone. A New York winter needs SPF 30; a Miami summer needs 50+. We recalculate for your coordinates every season.

03

Skin microbiome

Dry, oily, and sensitive skin each require different emollient and active balances. We adjust base formulation so the formula doesn't clog, pill, or irritate.

04

Seasonal reformulation

Your formula is compounded fresh each season — not stored on a shelf. Every batch reflects current UV forecasts and any skin changes you report.
